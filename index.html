Masters in Control System Engineering

In collaboration with Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO), Jadavpur University has designed a course in Master in control system engineering (MCSE) under the Electrical Engineering department from the year__. Under this curriculum, a dedicated advanced research laboratory has been set up for hands-on training on real-time research and development.
